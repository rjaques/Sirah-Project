The witness version of al-Ḥasan b. Ṣāliḥ b. Ṣāliḥ b. Ḥayy

السيرة النبوية لمحمد بن اسحاق في رواية الحسن بن صالح بن صالح



KHTSV04P307B

al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, al-Ismāʾ al-mubhamah fī al-anbāʾ al-muḥkamah, vol. 4 p. 307

وأخبرنا أبو الحسن علي بن أحمد بن عمر المقري قال أخبرنا أحمد بن محمد بن السري التميمي بالكوفة قال حدثنا المنذر بن محمد قال حدثنا أبي قال حدثنا @TR1_HSSH@ الحسن بن صالح عن محمد بن إسحاق عن عبد الملك بن أبي بكر عن محمد بن المنكدر عن جابر بن عبد الله قال جلد رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم نعيمان أربع مرات في الخمر فرأى المسلمون فرجا عظيما أن الحد قد وقع وأن القتل قد أخر.

COMMENT

HHMXV07P379A (ʿAbd al-Razzāq b. Hammām al-Ḥumayrī, al-Muṣannaf, vol. 7 p. 379) places this in the context of Ibn al-Nuʿmān and TKXXV03P375A (Ibn Saʿd, Ṭabaqāt al-kubrā, vol. 3 p. 375) quotes a portion of the passage in the biography of al-Nuʿmān b. ʿAmr (although he appears to suggest that Ibn Isḥāq names him as al-Nuʿaymān). TKXXV03P375A (Ibn Saʿd, Ṭabaqāt al-kubrā, vol. 3 p. 375) states that Nuʿaymān b. ʿAmr was the younger brother of Nuʿmān b. ʿAmr. Ibn al-Nuʿaymān or al-Nuʿaymān is not mentioned in the redaction of Ibn Hishām. He is sometimes identified as al-Nuʿaymān b. ʿAmr b. Rifāʿah b. al-Ḥārith. According to JWZXV05P277A (Ibn al-Jawzī, al-Muntaẓam fī Tārīkh al-mulūk waʾl-umam, vol. 5 p. 277), al-Nuʿaymān participated in Badr, and all of the famous battles in which Muḥammad fought. He was known to have repeatedly been punished for drinking nabīdh. BKHBV06P431A (Muḥammad b. Ismāʿīl b. Ibrāhīm al-Bukhārī, Taʾrīkh al-kabīr, vol. 6 p. 431) and TBRCV11P554A (al-Ṭabarī, Taʾrīkh al-rusul waʾl-mulūk, vol. 11 p. 554) mention the account of ʿUqbah b. al-Ḥārith where he describes being ordered by Muḥammad to beat Ibn Nuʿaymān or al-Nuʿaymān with shoes and palm branches for drinking. TKXXV03P375A (Ibn Saʿd, Ṭabaqāt al-kubrā, vol. 3 p. 375) states he died in during the caliphate of Muʿawīyah but KTHBV08P070A (Ibn Kathīr, al-Bidāyat waʾl-nihāyah, vol. 8 p. 70) places his death in the year 54. HJRSV06P353A (Ibn Ḥajar al-ʿAsqalānī, al-Iṣābah fī tamyīz al-ṣaḥābah, vol. 6 p. 353) states that Ibn Isḥāq identifies him as al-Nuʿmān b. ʿAmr and not as al-Nuʿaymān, this follows the comment of Ibn Hishām in vol. 1, 703A. A person identified as al-Nuʿmān b. ʿAmr is mention twice in the redaction of Ibn Hishām: on vol. 1, 552A he is identified as a Jew who was one of two Rabbis who questioned Muḥammad and then rejected him. On vol. 1, 703A he is identified as al-Nuʿmān b. ʿAmr b. Rifāʿah b. Sawād, a member of the Sawād b. Mālik b. Ghamm clan, among the Anṣ ār, who participated at Badr. Ibn Hishām, in his commentary, states his name was Nuʿaymān. It is not clear if Ibn Isḥāq understood al-Nuʿmān b. ʿAmr to be the individual who is commonly referred to as being punished for drinking. For passages within the Ibn Isḥāq tradition that identify Nuʿaymān or al-Nuʿaymān with the subject of the person punished for drinking, see via WSASN (Sharīk b. ʿAbd Allāh b. Abī Sharīk al-Najaʿī al-Kūfī): NSKXV05P143B (al-Nasāʾī, al-Sunan al-kubrā, vol. 5 p. 143) and IHMAV12P370B (Ibn Ḥāzim, al-Muḥallā bil-Āthār, vol. 12 p. 370); via the witness of WZATB (Ziyād b. ʿAbd Allāh b. al-Ṭufayl al-Kūfī al-Bakkāʾī al-ʿĀmirī) (but not found in the redaction of Ibn Hishām) NSSXV04P415A (Abū ʿAbd Allāh al-Ḥākim al-Nīsābūrī, al-Mustadrak ʿalā al-ṣaḥīḥīn, vol. 4 p. 415),IHMAV12P370C (Ibn Ḥāzim, al-Muḥallā bil-Āthār, vol. 12 p. 370), BQRXV03P340B (al-Bayhaqī, al-Sunan al-ṣaghīr, vol. 3 p. 340), BQSXV08P545B (al-Bayhaqī, Sunan al-kubrā, vol. 8 p. 545), and BQMXV13P039A (al-Bayhaqī, Maʿrifat al-sunan waʾl-āthār, vol. 13 p. 39); via the witness of WYUAU (Yaʿlā b. ʿUbayd b. Abī Umayyah al-Īyādī al-Tanāfisī al-Kūfī), IBABV01P081B ([REFERENCE NOT FOUND IN BIBLIOGRAPHY], vol. 1 p. 81), BQSXV08P545A (al-Bayhaqī, Sunan al-kubrā, vol. 8 p. 545), and KHTSV04P307A (al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, al-Ismāʾ al-mubhamah fī al-anbāʾ al-muḥkamah, vol. 4 p. 307); via WSACD (Ibrāhīm b. Saʿd b. Ibrāhīm b. ʿAbd al-Raḥmān al-Madinī al-Zuhrī): HBHXV32P210A (Aḥmad b. Ḥanbal, Musnad, vol. 32 p. 210); and via WHSSH (WITNESS NOT FOUND): KHTSV04P307B (al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, al-Ismāʾ al-mubhamah fī al-anbāʾ al-muḥkamah, vol. 4 p. 307) and YNBHV12P358D (al-ʿAynī, al-Bināyah sharḥ al-Hidāyah, vol. 12 p. 358).
