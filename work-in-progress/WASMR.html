SHBMV06P484A

Ibn Abī Shaybah, al-Kitāb al-muṣannaf fī al-aḥāddīth waʾl-āthār, vol. 6 p. 484

حدثنا @TR1_ASMR@ عبد الرحيم بن سليمان عن محمد بن إسحاق عن الزهري ومحمد بن علي عن يزيد بن هرمز قال كتب نجدة إلى ابن عباس يسأله عن قتل الولدان ويقول في كتابه إن العالم صاحب موسى قد قتل الوليد قال فقال يزيد أنا كتبت كتاب ابن عباس بيدي إلى نجدة إنك كتبت تسأل عن قتل الولدان وتقول في كتابك إن العالم صاحب موسى قد قتل الوليد ولو كنت تعلم من الولدان ما علم ذلك العالم من ذلك الوليد قتلته ولكنك لا تعلم قد نهى رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم عن قتلهم فاعتزلهم.

COMMENT

The passage is not found in HSNX. This is representative of a complex of traditions related by Yazīd b. Hurmuz about correspondence between Najdah al-Ḥarūrī and Ibn ʿAbbās in which Yazīd b. Hurmuz serves as Ibn ʿAbbās’ scribe. This fragment deals with the killing of children. Also see WASMR (ʿAbd al-Raḥīm b. Sulaymān al-Marwazī al-Rāzī), WMSAH (Muḥammad b. Salamah b. ʿAbd Allāh al-Bāhilī al-Ḥarrānī), and WYUAU (Yaʿlā b. ʿUbayd b. Abī Umayyah al-Īyādī al-Tanāfisī al-Kūfī).

Sulaymān al-Marwazī al-Rāzī), WAKMW (Aḥmad b. Khālid b. Mūsā al-Wahbī al-Kindī), WMSAH (Muḥammad b. Salamah b. ʿAbd Allāh al-Bāhilī al-Ḥarrānī), and WHAYA (Yazīd b. Hārūn al-Wāsiṭī).

THSHV02P126A

Aḥmad b. Muḥammad al-Ṭaḥāwī, Sharḥ mushkil al-āthār, vol. 2 p. 126-128

حدثنا إسحاق بن إبراهيم بن يونس البغدادي أبو يعقوب حدثنا الوليد بن شجاع أبو همام حدثنا @TR1_ASMR@ عبد الرحيم بن سليمان حدثنا محمد بن إسحاق عن ثور بن يزيد عن محمد بن عبيد قال بعثني عدي بن عدي الكندي إلى صفية بنت شيبة أسألها عن أشياء كانت ترويها عن عائشة فقالت حدثتني عائشة أنها سمعت رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم يقول لا عتاق ولا طلاق في إغلاق § وذكر البخاري هذا الحديث عن أحمد بن حنبل عن @TR2_SAII@ سعد بن § إبراهيم بن سعد عن @TR1_SACD@ أبيه عن ابن إسحاق عن ثور بن يزيد الكلاعي عن محمد بن عبيد بن أبي صالح المكي.

COMMENT

Passage not found in HSNX. The tradition is set during the ḥajj when ʿAdī b. ʿAdī al-Kindī advised Muḥammad b. ʿUbayd b. Abī Ṣāliḥ al-Makkī to go collect a tradition from Ṣafīyah bint Shaybah b. ʿUthmān b. Abī Ṭalḥah. Muḥammad b. ʿUbayd b. Abī Ṣāliḥ and ʿAdī b. ʿAdī al-Kindī are not mentioned in HSNX. Ṣafīyah bint Shaybah b. ʿUthmān is mentioned in HSNXV02P411C (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 2 p. 411) regarding the Conquest of Mecca (also see via WSACD (Ibrāhīm b. Saʿd b. Ibrāhīm b. ʿAbd al-Raḥmān al-Madinī al-Zuhrī) NYMXV06P3378A (Abū Nuʿaym, Maʿrifat al-ṣaḥābah, vol. 6 p. 3378)). Thawr b. Yazīd al-Kalāʿī, Abū Khālid, is mentioned three times by WZATB (Ziyād b. ʿAbd Allāh b. al-Ṭufayl al-Kūfī al-Bakkāʾī al-ʿĀmirī) (HSNXV01P166A (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 1 p. 166), HSNXV01P308A (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 1 p. 308), and HSNXV01P634C (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 1 p. 634)). According to Muḥammad b. Saʿd, he was from the people of Ḥimṣ and died in Jerusalem in 153/770 at the age of 60. His grandfather fought for Muʿāwiyah at Ṣiffīn against ʿAlī (TKXXV07P324A (Ibn Saʿd, Ṭabaqāt al-kubrā, vol. 7 p. 324)). Muḥammad b. ʿUbayd b. Abī Ṣāliḥ al-Makkī is mentioned in BKHBV01P171C (Muḥammad b. Ismāʿīl b. Ibrāhīm al-Bukhārī, Taʾrīkh al-kabīr, vol. 1 p. 171), HJKXV01P583A (Muslim b. al-Ḥajjāj, al-Kunan waʾl-asmāʾ, vol. 1 p. 583), BNTHV07P371C (Ibn Ḥibbān, al-Thiqāt, vol. 7 p. 371), and MZTXV19P215A (al-Mizzī, Tahdhīb al-kamāl fī asmāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 19 p. 215) only in relation to this tradition, although the latter source states that he also related from Mujāhid b. Jabr al-Makkī and in addition to Thawr b. Yazīd al-Ḥimṣī, he also related to Mujāhid b. Jabr al-Makkī. According to HJRBV09P330A (Ibn Ḥajar al-ʿAsqalānī, Tahdhīb al-Tahdhīb, vol. 9 p. 330) he lived in Jerusalem. No date of death is mentioned. ʿAdī b. ʿAdī b. ʿUmayrah al-Kindī al-Shaybānī, Abū Farwah al-Jazīrī, was a member of the al-Ḥārith b. Muʿāwiyah b. Thawr tribe. Described as pious by al-Ṭabarī, he commanded troops under Muḥammad b. Marwān against Khārijī rebels in 76/ 695 (TBRBV06P243A (al-Ṭabarī, Taʾrīkh al-rusul waʾl-mulūk (1967), vol. 6 p. 243)). He is described as a partisan of the Umayyads and was appointed as commander of Mūsil, the Jazīrah, and then Armīnīyah by ʿUmar b. ʿAbd al-ʿAzīz (TDSHV40P137A (Ibn ʿAsākir, Taʾrīkh madīnat Dimashq, vol. 40 p. 137), although MZTXV19P5354B (al-Mizzī, Tahdhīb al-kamāl fī asmāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 19 p. 5354) states this was done by Sulaymān b. ʿAbd al-Mālik). He was included among the 3rd generation of successors in the Jazīrah and to have been trustworthy (ibid., 138). He died in 120/738 according to al-Dhahabī and others (DHBRV01P116D (al-Dhahabī, al-ʿIbar fī khabar man ghabar, vol. 1 p. 116)). Ṣafīyah bint Shaybah b. ʿUthmān b. Abī Ṭalḥah was a Meccan companion and related from many of the Prophet's wives and related to many in the first generation of successors. No date of death is mentioned (see, MZTXV35P212B (al-Mizzī, Tahdhīb al-kamāl fī asmāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 35 p. 212)). One of the most comonly quoted traditions in the Ibn Isḥāq corpus. Passage also found in WAANH (ʿAbd Allāh b. Numayr al-Hamdānī al-Khāraqī al-Kūfī), WZAZA (Zakarīyā b. Abī Zāʾidah), and WSACD (Ibrāhīm b. Saʿd b. Ibrāhīm b. ʿAbd al-Raḥmān al-Madinī al-Zuhrī). Unable to place the date of the event in which the tradition was collected in Mecca. Most likely to be placed in the opinions of Muḥammad in fiqh section.
