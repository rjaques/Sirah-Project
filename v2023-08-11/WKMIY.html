The witness version of Karāmah bint Muḥammad b. Isḥāq b. Yasār

سيرة النبوية لمحمد بن اسحاق في روية كرامة بنت محمد بن اسحاق بن يسار

محمد بن إسحاق بن يسار المطلبي



NYMXV05P2808A

Abū Nuʿaym, Maʿrifat al-ṣaḥābah, vol. 5 p. 2808

أخبرنا محمد بن محمد بن يعقوب النيسابوري في كتابه ثنا محمد بن محمد بن سليمان ثنا جعفر بن عبد الواحد قال قال لي محمد بن إسحاق بن كثير بن يسار حدثتني @TR1_KMIY@ كرامة بنت محمد بن إسحاق بن يسار عن أبيها محمد عن أبيه إسحاق عن جده يسار أنه أتى النبي صلى الله عليه وسلم فمسح رأسه ودعا له بالبركة.

COMMENT

The identity of Muḥammad b. Isḥāq b. Kathīr b. Yasār is unknown and he is only listed in conjunction with this passage in the pre-modern corpus. TDSHV69P189B (Ibn ʿAsākir, Taʾrīkh madīnat Dimashq, vol. 69 p. 189) lists a Muḥammad b. Isḥāq b. Kathīr al-Ṣawrī, who apparently gave dictation to Abū al-Ḥasan Khaythumah b. Sulaymān and heard from Mūʾmmal b. Ismāʿīl. The editor of the text states that his name may have been Muḥammad b. Ibrāhīm b. Kathīr. BNTHV09P144F (Ibn Ḥibbān, al-Thiqāt, vol. 9 p. 144) gives this name but does not list a date of death. TDSHV46P301B (Ibn ʿAsākir, Taʾrīkh madīnat Dimashq, vol. 46 p. 301), however, gives his name as Abū al-Ḥasan Muḥammad b. Ibrāhīm b. Kathīr b. Wafdān al-Ṣawrī, which would seem to eliminate him as the possible source. Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī gives two variations of the name: Muḥammad b. Ibrāhīm b. Kathīr, Abū ʿAbd Allāh al-Ṣayfūrī who lived at the Syria Gate in Baghdād around 273/886 (see KHTGV02P283A (al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, Taʾrīkh Baghdād, vol. 2 p. 283)) and a Muḥammad b. Ibrāhīm b. Kathīr al-Ṣawrī but gives not biographical details (see KHTGV08P665A (al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, Taʾrīkh Baghdād, vol. 8 p. 665)). According to KHTGV07P623A (al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, Taʾrīkh Baghdād, vol. 7 p. 623), Jaʿfar b. ʿAbd al-Wāḥid’s kunyā was al-Hāshimī and Muḥammad b. Muḥammad b. Sulaymān is listed as al-Bāghandī. JJKXV02P396D (al-Jurjānī, al-Kāmil fī ḍuʿafāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 2 p. 396) states that Jaʿfar b. ʿAbd al-Wāḥid related from Wahb b. Jarīr, the son of the witness Jarīr b. Ḥāzim. He was also appointed Qāḍī of al-Baṣrah (KHRXV01P328B (al-Khalīlī, al-Irshād fī maʿrifah ʿulamāʾ al-ḥadīth, vol. 1 p. 328)). He was also appointed chief judge of Rāʾy in 240/854 and died in 258/871 (KHTGV08P55A (al-Khaṭīb al-Baghdādī, Taʾrīkh Baghdād, vol. 8 p. 55)).
