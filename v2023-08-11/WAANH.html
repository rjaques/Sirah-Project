SHBMV07P414E

Ibn Abī Shaybah, al-Kitāb al-muṣannaf fī al-aḥāddīth waʾl-āthār, vol. 7 p. 414

@TR1_AANH@ عبد الله بن نمير قال حدثنا محمد بن إسحاق عن عبد الرحمن بن القاسم عن أبيه عن عائشة قالت لما أتت وفاة جعفر عرفنا في وجه رسول الله صلى الله عليه وسلم الحزن قالت فدخل عليه رجل فقال يا رسول الله إن النساء يبكين قال فارجع إليهن فأسكتهن فإن أبين فاحث في وجوههن التراب قال قالت عائشة قلت في نفسي والله ما تركت نفسك ولا أنت مطيع رسول الله.

Abī Ṣāliḥ and ʿAdī b. ʿAdī al-Kindī are not mentioned in HSNX. Ṣafīyah bint Shaybah b. ʿUthmān is mentioned in HSNXV02P411C (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 2 p. 411) regarding the Conquest of Mecca (also see via WSACD (Ibrāhīm b. Saʿd b. Yazīd al-Kalāʿī, Abū Khālid, is mentioned three times by WZATB (Ziyād b. ʿAbd Allāh b. al-Ṭufayl al-Kūfī al-Bakkāʾī al-ʿĀmirī) (HSNXV01P166A (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 1 p. 166), HSNXV01P308A (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 1 p. 308), and HSNXV01P634C (ʿAbd al-Malik b. Hishām, al-Sīrah al-Nabuwīyah, vol. 1 p. 634)). According to Muḥammad b. Saʿd, he was from the people of Ḥimṣ and died in Jerusalem in 153/770 at the age of 60. His grandfather fought for Muʿāwiyah at Ṣiffīn against ʿAlī (TKXXV07P324A (Ibn Saʿd, Ṭabaqāt al-kubrā, vol. 7 p. 324)). Muḥammad b. ʿUbayd b. Abī Ṣāliḥ al-Makkī is mentioned in BKHBV01P171C (Muḥammad b. Ismāʿīl b. Ibrāhīm al-Bukhārī, Taʾrīkh al-kabīr, vol. 1 p. 171), HJKXV01P583A (Muslim b. al-Ḥajjāj, al-Kunan waʾl-asmāʾ, vol. 1 p. 583), BNTHV07P371C (Ibn Ḥibbān, al-Thiqāt, vol. 7 p. 371), and MZTXV19P215A (al-Mizzī, Tahdhīb al-kamāl fī asmāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 19 p. 215) only in relation to this tradition, although the latter source states that he also related from Mujāhid b. Jabr al-Makkī and in addition to Thawr b. Yazīd al-Ḥimṣī, he also related to Mujāhid b. Jabr al-Makkī. According to HJRBV09P330A (Ibn Ḥajar al-ʿAsqalānī, Tahdhīb al-Tahdhīb, vol. 9 p. 330) he lived in Jerusalem. No date of death is mentioned. ʿAdī b. ʿAdī b. ʿUmayrah al-Kindī al-Shaybānī, Abū Farwah al-Jazīrī, was a member of the al-Ḥārith b. Muʿāwiyah b. Thawr tribe. Described as pious by al-Ṭabarī, he commanded troops under Muḥammad b. Marwān against Khārijī rebels in 76/ 695 (TBRBV06P243A (al-Ṭabarī, Taʾrīkh al-rusul waʾl-mulūk (1967), vol. 6 p. 243)). He is described as a partisan of the Umayyads and was appointed as commander of Mūsil, the Jazīrah, and then Armīnīyah by ʿUmar b. ʿAbd al-ʿAzīz (TDSHV40P137A (Ibn ʿAsākir, Taʾrīkh madīnat Dimashq, vol. 40 p. 137), although MZTXV19P5354B (al-Mizzī, Tahdhīb al-kamāl fī asmāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 19 p. 5354) states this was done by Sulaymān b. ʿAbd al-Mālik). He was included among the 3rd generation of successors in the Jazīrah and to have been trustworthy (ibid., 138). He died in 120/738 according to al-Dhahabī and others (DHBRV01P116D (al-Dhahabī, al-ʿIbar fī khabar man ghabar, vol. 1 p. 116)).

Ṣafīyah bint Shaybah b. ʿUthmān b. Abī Ṭalḥah was a Meccan companion and related from many of the Prophet's wives and related to many in the first generation of successors. No date of death is mentioned (see, MZTXV35P212B (al-Mizzī, Tahdhīb al-kamāl fī asmāʾ al-rijāl, vol. 35 p. 212)). One of the most comonly quoted traditions in the Ibn Isḥāq corpus. Passage also found in WSACD (Ibrāhīm b. Saʿd b. Ibrāhīm b. ʿAbd al-Raḥmān al-Madinī al-Zuhrī), WZAZA (Zakarīyā b. Abī Zāʾidah), and WASMR (ʿAbd al-Raḥīm b. Sulaymān al-Marwazī al-Rāzī). Unable to place the date of the event in which the tradition was collected in Mecca. Most likely to be placed in the opinions of Muḥammad in fiqh section.
